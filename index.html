Intelligence Architecture

Not a bulletin board.
A thinking platform.

Every existing tender platform shows you what is already public. Trovally shows you what matters — before the market prices it in. Six layers of intelligence running simultaneously every day.

01 —
Live Opportunity Feed
Government tenders, grants and funding programs crawled daily from official portals. Auto-classified, plain language summaries, expired entries automatically archived.
02 —
Legislative Radar
New financial laws and regulations monitored within hours of publication. Plain language interpretation of what each change means for deal makers before their advisors call them back.
03 —
Development Finance
World Bank, AfDB, IDB, ADB and regional development banks in one place. The highest value opportunities most deal makers never see because nobody assembled them before.
04 —
Pattern Detection
When a government enters a spending cycle the AI flags it before the market recognises it. Three infrastructure tenders in 30 days signals a cycle. You move on intelligence not news.
05 —
Personalised Alerts
Your sector. Your countries. Your opportunity size. The platform learns your profile and surfaces what is relevant before you search. Urgency alerts at 7 days and 48 hours before deadline.
06 —
Archive Intelligence
Expired opportunities become intelligence not dead links. Search three years of what a government funded. Understand patterns. Build stronger proposals with full historical context.
